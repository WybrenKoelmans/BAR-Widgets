# Top Bar Extra

**Description:** Adds a more detailed resource display.

Also factored in Resources from Team Overflow. I will show up as a number on your Share slider and included in your total if receiving (but excluded if sending).
This simple but essential widget will show you a balance for your Metal/Energy. You can easily spot if it is time to build a Converter (+70e T1/+600e T2). 

The value is smoothed over a period of time, to avoid the jumps up and down as your units/buildings finish, so you will get a good feeling for how your economy is doing.
