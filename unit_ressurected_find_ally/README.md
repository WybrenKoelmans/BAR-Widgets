# Ressurected Find Ally

**Description:** Resurrected units will find their ally who resurrected them or a building.

So instead of standing around in the middle of no-mans-land, they will fall back to the closest ally and guard them. This ends up if you have some resurrectors following your army, the zombies will join the attack automatically.

Resurrected resurrectors (pfff) will automatically join resurrecting. Try saying that 5 times in a row.

It also helps if you are resurrecting in front of your frontline, as the zombies will automatically retreat to your frontline.
