# Adaptive Graphics

**Description:** Automatically adjusts your graphics settings based on your FPS.

This widget will automatically adjust your settings based on your FPS (you can set a target framerate in Custom tab settings). 

**To use**:
Start a game, and setup your target FPS in the Custom tab of the in game options menu.
Setup the regular graphics preset based on your preference, this widget will not adjust settings that require a restart of the game so make sure to set those yourself.
Start playing a game! If you are getting higher FPS than your target, the widget will start making it look much fancier!
As the game progresses and things get heavier on your GPU, your FPS will drop below your target, and the widget will start to scale down your graphics.

The widget is very log heavy at the moment, but i need it because if you have complaints, I need to see what happened in your logs.

Note that your graphics is usually not the worst part of low FPS (usually your CPU will be the bottleneck in large games). But it won't hurt.
