# Factory Always-Alt Queue

**Description:** Always queue certain units (e.g. constructors) with alt (priority) in factories.

This widget will automatically do a ALT+Queue command instead of a repeating queue for units that don't make sense to repeat.

There are very little games I want some units on repeat, and lets face it, in the heat of battle you accidentally turn on repeat with that jammer still in your queue and when you scroll back to your base you are met with 30 jammers instead of 30 sharpshooters.
