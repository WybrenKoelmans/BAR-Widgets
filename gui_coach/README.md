# Coach

**Description:** Gives a list of things you should probably do to play well.

This widget will analyse your current sitation and make suggestions (most important on top) on what would be a pretty good thing to do in most situations.

It can help beginners understand how to scale economy and when to expand their army or invest more in eco. It also helps to understand stalling (both metal/energy) and how to use converters.

Note that this is made by a OS 15 player, so don't expect to turn into a Pro running this, just that it might get you from OS5 to OS15+

If you are playing games with custom widgets disabled, you can still see the advice during the replay, seeing what you might have wanted to do different at that point.

Try it out!
