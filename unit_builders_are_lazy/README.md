# Builders Are Lazy

**Description:** Builders will idle instead of guarding after building (to become available from the idle mechanic)

Has this ever happened to you?
Add a construction bot to your repeat queue on accident and now you have 40 construction bots guarding your T1 Bot lab?
Or maybe 8 bots standing around doing nothing next to the commander guarding him after repairing?
Or accidentally ordered two bots to guard each other, these are lost forever (slackers)!

I am a noob, and these things happen to me all the time.

NO MORE! Introducing lazy builders: they do only do what you tell them to do and then go back to Idle (so they are easy to find again with CTRL+Tab). Ready to be reassigned to a useful job. They will also not guard the factory (but will stay next to the factory  if you enable Guard Factory) Always a nice Idle notification right after your new bot is ready!

The default "Guard" behavior is changed to "Repair" if the unit is not full HP, or "Move" when the thing you are clicking does not need building or repairing.
Manually issuing guard commands is still possible of course.

Please note that this is my first Widget, so please provide any comment you think is useful.

Important: This widget will disable two of the base game widgets called "Idle Constructor Guard After Build" and "Guard damaged constructors" as they have conflicting behavior with this widget. If you wish to use the behavior of these widgets, please uninstall this widget
