# Smart Mines

**Description:** Mines will detonate only when an enemy of appropriate tech level is nearby.

Mines are not used in a lot games, and medium and heavy mines even less. I suspect it because they kill mostly ticks!

Using this widget, medium mines will only detonate for T2/T3 and heavy mines will detonate only for T3.

It can make a good early T2 rush stopper, or a T3 pack of fast assault straight into a minefield (without having to worry to waste your expensive mines on ticks and grunts).
